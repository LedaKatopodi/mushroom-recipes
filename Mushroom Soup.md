# Mushroom Soup

## 🧅 Ingredients

- [ ] 10 garlic cloves, minced
- [ ] 1 white onion, diced
- [ ] 3 packs (~250g each) mushrooms, chopped (suggested mix: shiitake, portobello, white, wild, **_not_** amanitas)
- [ ] Beef stock as needed (~1L usually); use vegetable stock/broth for vegan/vegetarian version
- [ ] 60g butter
- [ ] White wine
- [ ] 1 bunch of thyme
- [ ] Soy sauce
- [ ] (Coconut) cream
- [ ] Salt, pepper, paprika (smoked)

## 🎞️ Instructions

1. Sauté the diced onion in butter. Add garlic and the seasonings.
2. Add mushrooms and cook down.
3. Add white wine until it has evaporated. Cook through for 5 minutes.
4. Add stock. Bunch up thyme and bundle it together with twine, and add it to the pot.
5. Cover and let simmer at medium-low heat for at least 30 minutes.
6. Add soy sauce and cream. Adjust salt and seasonings.
7. Blend for a bit with the hand blender, but without blending everything (leave some chunks for the "crunch").
